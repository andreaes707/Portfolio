Multiple Reasons to grow..

I write blogs for multiple reasons....
To:
  • Bring everyone the knowledge &/or resources they seek;
  • Thoroughly spread awareness about the voices who remain unheard & unnoticed;
  • Expand my knowledge and experience, so I can continue to help others using guidance and wisdom not obtained soley from myself..
  • Share any knowledge I come across that feels important to share, since it is not exclusively for myself
  • Eventually obtain some income to support me, my family & their future, as well as to support my current and future communities.

I know this page and many blogs will have a lot of quotes but each quote has a unique purpose.
A message to be spread; for everyone to hear; for anyone to listen; & to those whom never wondered before..

Honesty, respect and compassion:

I have chosen to live with compassion for all living things. Additionally, I have chosen to live with a heart filled with light, positivity and possibilities.

That does not mean that my life has only been full of positivity.

For positivity and negativity have an unseen balance;

An equal connection of harmony between cause and effect.

Where one exists, so does the other.

Be truthful and honest, (even) especially in your weakest moments you can find an advantage with the truth.

There is no good in this world without the real will to do good.

Integrity

I am inspired to help the world understand some important things;

Like absolutely having, (recognizing and using integrity regularly) can make a world of difference for multiple communities, not just us as individuals.

We can also learn to be less egotistical and more modest, respectful, full of truth and less arrogant.

I have adapted and learned to make any & every attempt to find inspiration in everyday life.

Having inspiration can make compassionate actions simpler and more enjoyable.

Especially since not everyone can seem to find that daily inspiration, even for their hardest work.

They only need to realize that it has been lurking unnoticed within sight, as they conduct business, travel, work, play & love every day.

One must enjoy what their actions and their life serves them and to find the inspiration to better themselves and help others when they can.

Curiosity and creativity give a flow to all that I do and that gives all the more meaning to everything that inspires me.

I could not imagine my life without such beautiful inspiration since it is so very meaningful to me;

At times, it keeps me on my toes too!

A simple way I find inspiration is from many quotes.

Imagination is the highest form of research. ~ Albert Einstein

To lose patience is to lose the battle.

~ MAHATMA GANDHI

Two things define you: Your patience when you have nothing, and your attitude when you have everything.

Patience & Balance


Patience

Have patience for time is the only permanent key to happiness and success.

Balance

Weigh your choices :

Choose what's best for your needs, not your wants;

No matter what each positive action comes back around; Especially when you need it most..

Same goes for those negative actions; Except they come around when least expected; As a surprise no doubt...

All remains good when one remains in good standing(harmony) with the balance of the universe.

Lucky or Not:

We each individually strive to aim for success.

Success for our families and ourselves.

You get what you give and you give for the betterment of everyone. You give what you can when you can.

Even when you are deprived of a need the right time will come for it to be fulfilled.

In place of a repercussion you might earn or receive a karmic reward for your efforts....

Although time is a construct made by man, we depend on it for optimal functionality, and a great way to observe the days that turn into years and then decades.
