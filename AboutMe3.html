Life Lessons & Choices

I have been alive 20 years now, two decades worth of choices. Choices that were beneficial, good decisions or not, sometimes choices hurt no matter what.

Our choices and mistakes are a part of life, a part of who we are yet they do not directly define us and they certainly do not confine us.

Table of Contents

  • Intro

Observation

The first 10 years was just observing, practicing and mastering the skills I knew, but when we do realize that we have the option: at a point in our life, we can choose to keep pursuing the path of learning or we stick to a specific skill set acquired by youth through parents & mentors.

Easy said when someone is young and already used to learning almost every day (probably every day if they find it fun or interesting).

Older young-adults don't wanna learn not just because it is tough effort but it also is rather exhausting for the mentality of their age group and level of experience.

Everyone can choose to learn again only if they believe they can and actually want to experience learning again.

Imagination and Negativity Release

I feel like my imagination allows me to remain in good judgement when problems arise. Imagination allows so many possibilities and fantasies to emerge into the world through our minds, hands and bodies.

I use my free time to constantly meditate, relax, and doodle to keep my energies in harmonious balance.

Wisdom does not ever just appear

Wisdom ultimately comes from patience, trust, respect in equilibrium.

Remaining calm, understanding your best virtues and letting your intuition be a positive guide. Wisdom comes from experience (not just your own) and well learnt lessons come to us throughout life.

Being cognizant to all around you, no harmful judgement.

Love & Compassion All The Way

I am a lifelong student of many studies and I love nothing more than my family, my communities, and mother nature as well as all who sustain Earth. I am creatively passionate about everything I do, from home to work.

Life lessons need to be learnt, even when they broke me I continued directly forward. Onward through a path filled with light among all the darkness.(For a long time I could only see the darkness) I AM Loyal only to the brightness of love forever, wherever I go love & compassion go with me.

When I was lost I barely found the support I needed to survive but all was not completely lost. Those decisions we have the option to make are our building blocks, and other vast important life pieces.

Every time I have become deeply lost or confused in this world, someone has always been there when I needed it most and I could not be thankful enough.

Having Faith

..Makes Surviving & understanding those Life Lessons Better

I have had my fair share of life problems, not having important pieces had destroyed my life at one point, it became a literal pile of shit. And I was young and for having a whole future ahead of me it wasn't the best thing that the whole train derailed before I even reached adulthood.

I had some hope, enough faith to survive and with the proper support, my decision to continue living normal life happened, this is not where or when I explain my full story but our pasts help us get to the future once we accept what happened & observe what is, as well as acknowledging our hardships.

We must also be willing to move on and keep renewing our faith in the world, renewing our ability to adapt, to accept different perspectives, be more compassionate, find a way to actually understand what is important.

We choose who we are, who we want to be, what we will reach for, our achievements, our experiences, our path through the growth of life.

The next few paragraphs describe how emotions affect us a little, some of my current struggles, and then I explain what my studies and hobbies are.

At our center we not only have a soul, but a body capable of intense uncontrollable emotions. Emotions, feelings we feel every day, we grow up with them, we learn them, learn with them, misinterpret them and the cycle continues. They directly impact our actions, help us figure out what is right or wrong. What is simply nonsense and what actually makes sense.

While I was younger (around 15) I had basic life responsibilities to deal with and I lived independently (when it came to surviving day-to-day I was doing most of it alone or with friends). As well as through the support of my parents as well as other family and friends. A lot of my survival was common sense and the knowledge I gained throughout my childhood.

Through that time while remaining fairly healthy and nearly healed in the physical way, I was going through a ton of severe mental issues from various traumatic incidents and even I know that some incidents could have been avoided.

Not saying that any should have been avoided due to their valuable lessons. Every mistake made has contributed to giving me new knowledge and new perspectives to become a better and more aware person.

Those various incidents are a few different stories to be shared within the reasonable future. Overall every incident counted toward making me more committed and determined to be the best entrepreneur, stranger, friend, teacher, mother, companion, wife, and philosopher. Although the hell I went through wasn't a physical problem at first it has now become a problem in the form of an unknown epileptic disorder that has been controlling parts of my life for over 2 years now.

I have finally broken through my severe problems and I finally get the chance to properly rehabilitate my mind and body, for me this gives me a new start to an exciting journey.

Given the circumstances, my life has not ever been this stable. But my terms of stable are actually quite unstable since my home is my family. My family are my life whether or not we have food, clothes, a roof or other luxuries.

Life is apparently a luxury that can not be afforded even with all the love you have to give. I have always appreciated clothes, food and a roof more than anything else when I needed it most. Some helped when even they couldn't afford to, putting themselves at risk for someone as broken as I was.

Truly faith, respect and patience kept me & my shrinking faith alive. Those who all gave honestly, help, and wisdom, helped me become who I really am. I currently keep surprising myself with my ability to keep up with the daily rehabilitation of my body and mind.

No matter how much people disagree or disrespect you, their judgement can not ever harm you. Those words they say only express their opinion not the facts that matter.

I am a rare butterfly; I have chosen to express myself however I feel within the constraints of positivity & integrity. Kindly kill your enemies with compassion and love on your journey to finding your true self.

If you choose to bury yourself in your cocoon sooner than most you best be sure to always let all your true colors shine. Emerge from the darkness brighter than you could have imagined before your transformation.

I am a young passionate philosopher, a lover of wisdom, learning, understanding, honesty, and loyalty who loves to use imagination and curiosity actively in daily life.

Using my imagination daily allows me to maintain complete positivity and remain calm in various situations. I am driven by using my integrity which to me means I have to use the truth, be honest and be respectful, all in an equal balance.

My beliefs also inspire my enthusiasm and optimism for everything I do. I am compassionate to all living things since they are all a part of nature. Nature is the beauty of our physical world. We must do what we can to nurture and promote health to all living things.

What do I believe in?

I am open-minded enough to believe in many different religions, rituals, and cultures. Each one is unique and spiritually serves a "higher purpose" within Earth, connecting our world with worlds next to us. I love philosophy and astronomy and I'm empowered to seek out the truth and understand everything fascinating in our world

When talking about music, all I can really say is that my world is "driven" by music. Music allows immense concentration to all tasks especially when work becomes problematic.
